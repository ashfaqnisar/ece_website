About Us

About VJIT

Vidya Jyothi Institute of Technology (VJIT) was established in 1999. Situated in Aziznagar. VJIT has a sprawling and lush green campus with architecturally splendid buildings in an area of 12 acres. The college has got a rich library of books, Internet lab and modern labs for each Department. The college is accredited by NAAC & NBA of AICTE. The college prides on the fact that it has a very senior and highly accomplished faculty- one of the best in the region. VJIT is rated as one of the top ten engineering colleges in Telangana.

About ECE Department

The department of ECE is a major strength to the institute. We have an excellent group of faculties who have lots of teaching and research experience. The department of ECE has MTech / BTech with an intake of M.Tech 36/B.Tech 240 seats. The department has well equipped laboratories.

We nurture the young talent available in the country and transform them into enterprising technologists so that they can contribute immensely to the technological development and prosperity of the country and provide dynamic leadership to others. The department is also well equipped with exclusive research labs like NI Labview center of excellence, ARM University Program & Cypress PSOC Semiconductors Laboratory.

About ICICI

The main objective of ICICI 2020 is to provide an opportunity for the participants to discuss and exchange the recent developments in the field of electronics and also review the challenges faced by the community in the 21st century. This conference will offer the scholars, academicians, scientists, and practitioners from engineering colleges, universities, and R&D institutes to share the updated advancements and solutions for the most important issues faced by the society.

The conference consists of presentations, posters and will also cover a wide range of topics and issues based on specific themes. Students are persuaded with a significant reduced registration fee. Best posters will be judged and awarded during the conference.
